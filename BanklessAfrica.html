Web Migration:From Drag and Drop Builder to Code-Based Website Transition

This was a collaborative effort that successfully transitioned Bankless Africa's website from a WordPress platform to a JavaScript-based website within the BanklessDAO media node. In this role, my responsibilities centered on implementing the technical aspects of the migration, focusing on the intricate process of transforming the website's structure and functionality. The decision to shift away from WordPress was driven by a strategic need to enhance functionality, providing the website with increased customization and adaptability in the rapidly evolving landscape of cryptocurrency media. The adoption of a JavaScript framework aimed to not only elevate the website's performance but also to empower the team with the flexibility to integrate cutting-edge features and capabilities, ensuring Bankless Africa remains at the forefront of innovation in the crypto space.
